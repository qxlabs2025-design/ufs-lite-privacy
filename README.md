Privacy Policy for Ultimate Flight Simulator-Lite
Last updated: February 13, 2026
Ultimate Flight Simulator-Lite is an offline flight simulation game. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how user data is handled.
Information We Collect
Username (Pilot Name)
The app allows users to enter a name (such as a pilot name or nickname) for personalization purposes within the game interface.
The name is used only for in-game display
The name is stored locally on the user’s device
The name is not transmitted over the internet
The name is not collected, stored, or accessed by the developer
The name is not shared with any third parties
Providing a name is optional and is used only to personalize the gameplay experience.
Device or Other IDs
The app may access basic device or app-generated identifiers that are required for normal app functionality, compatibility, performance, or stability.
These identifiers are not linked to personal identity
They are not transmitted over the internet
They are not used for tracking or advertising
They are not shared with the developer or third parties
Data Transmission
Ultimate Flight Simulator-Lite works fully offline.
The app does not transmit any user data over the internet.
Data Sharing
We do not share user data with third parties.
Data Retention
All data used by the app remains only on the user’s device and is removed when:
The app is uninstalled, or
The app data is cleared by the user
Accounts
The app does not support account creation, login, or authentication of any kind.
Children’s Privacy
This app is intended for users aged 13 and older.
The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
